  • Hotel San Martin

  • $500/night

  • San Martin Hotel is a colonial-style luxury hotel in Rosario. It was established by Ramiro Montez, the Riviera Brothers, in 1887. The hotel was named after the San Martin General, considered as a national hero in the country. It is the flagship property of Riviera Hotels & Resorts, a subsidiary of Riviera San Martin Hotels International.

  • Pellegrini Hotel

  • $100/night

  • Designed by architect Oscar Garcia Lourdes, at a height of 226 meters it is one of Rosario's tallest hotels. Situated at 1200 Pellegrini Avenue, the hotel sits next to Parque Independencia. The 5-star hotel has 1,261 rooms and suites, 16 restaurants and bars, a Convention Centre, and one of Rosario's largest spas.
